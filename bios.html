TheAndroids
The Androids (16, 17, and 18) are all creations of Dr. Gero. All the Androids are robots which mean they never get tired. Android 16 later became part of the Z gang, Android 18 also did but Android 17 still remained a bad guy. After being tired of being a wimp, Dr. Gero made himself a android, Android 20. Android 17 & 18 got mad at him so they killed him but just before he died, he made a last command for his newest creation, Android 21, also called Cell.


Bulma
Bulma is one of the most stubborn characters in Dragonballz, she is the first girl Goku has seen. She is also one of the most brilliant people in Dragonballz. She has saved the day a few times. Bulma can fix almost anything. She has fixed Raditz's scouter, the Dragonball radar, a spaceship, and much more. She is the wife of Vegeta and the mother of Trunks.

Buu
This fat, ugly, pink tub-a-lard may look dumb but he was one of the most powerful guys in Dragonballz. Buu was made by Dabura. In one part, he destroyed the world which killed almost all the Z fighters. He had commanded his zombies to suck up Gohan's power for himself. Only Gotenks was able to stand up to Buu. Buu, like the others can also change into different forms, each making him stronger. In one part Buu split in two, one good one evil. When Goku destroyed the evil one with his spirit bomb, the good one was spat out and became part of the Z force.

Cell
Cell (android 21) was created by Dr. Gero (Android 20). Cell was the last Android to be made, he is also the strongest. Dr. Gero had commanded him to destroy the other Androids so Cell did.
After devouring many humans, he became a real person instead of a Android because he ate so many people. He also ate the three androids which made him stronger.

Chauzu
Looking like a baby and acting is just normal for Chauzu. But even by his looks, he's not young, in fact, he's older than Goku!!! Chauzu is one of the weaker people of the Z gang. Chauzu is Tien's sidekick.

Chichi
Chichi is one of the most meanest characters in Dragonballz. She always tells Gohan to do his work. She also cares about his safety. She is the wife of Goku and mother of Gohan & Goten.

Frieza
The first strong villian of Dragonballz is Frieza. Many people feared Frieza. In fact some people went on her team so they wont be beat up. Frieza first went to Planet Namek to gather the Dragonballs so he can make his wish. At that time, no one in the universe can take on Frieza.
When Goku killed Frieza in Super Saiyan, Frieza became a half-robot and faced the Z gang once more, but got beat again.

Gogeta
Gogeta is made up of Goku and Vegeta combined. You can actually tell that their combined because of the name, Go... Goku and ...geta Vegeta. Gogeta is only in DragonballGT, when Goku fused with Vegeta the second time to fight Evil Shenlong.

Gohan
Gohan is one of the more powerful fighters in the gang. Son of Goku & Chi-chi and trained by Piccolo, Gohan was the hero many times including the times when he bounced the spirit bomb to Vegeta and when he blew up Cell. As he grew up, he started posing his father. Gohan is named after Goku's Grandfather (Grandpa Gohan). Gohan, like his father, is a Saiyan too which means he can go into Super Saiyan levels.

Goku
The strongest of the pack and the main character best describes Goku. Goku is the husband of Chichi and is father of Gohan & Goten. He has saved the day one million times. He eats a lot (but he's still skinny). Goku was the first person to reach SSJ1, SSJ3, and SSJ4. He's the one that defeated Raditz (with the help of Piccolo), defeated Vegeta, defeated Buu, defeated Baby, and the Shenlongs.

Goten
Goten is Goku's son and Gohan's little brother. He is also Trunk's best friend. Without Goten, Trunks would have never fused with him which then Gotenks wouldn't be formed, without Gotenks there's no SSJ3 Gotenks to hold on to Buu and Buu would have ruled the universe!!! Goten is only one year younger than Trunks.

Gotenks
Goten & Trunks make up Gotenks. Gotenks is one of the three fusion characters beside Gogeta & Vegetto. Gotenks is also the youngest one of the three. Gotenks & Goku are the only ones who reached to SSJ3. When they did that, their hair grew four feet!!!

Krillin
Krillin is the funnyest character because he's short, he doesnt have that white stuff in his eyes, he doesnt have a nose, he has a bald head, and he always gets kicked around. But as they say, "dont judge a book by it's cover," Krillin is strong! He has saved the day many times.

Master Roshi
Master Roshi is the Master of Martial arts. He is Goku's first fighting teacher. He was the once the strongest person in the world at one time. When Grandpa Gohan died, Master Roshi raised Goku till he is what he is now.

Piccolo
Goku's first enemy is Piccolo. Piccolo is a descendant of Kami which means he's a Namek. Piccolo became on the good side when Raditz killed Goku and Piccolo wanted help fighting the oncoming Saiyans. When Nappa shot at Gohan, Piccolo remembered his times training Gohan and remembered that he would protect him so he lunged forward and blocked the shot saving Gohan but killing himself. He later got wished back.

Raditz
The first villan in Dragonballz. He's also Goku's brother. When he arrived, he was seeking for Kakorot (Goku) for deserting the Saiyan army for so many years. He became enemies with Piccolo. Goku attacked Raditz but it was no use so both Goku and Raditz became enemies of Raditz. At the end, Goku had to sacrifice his life in order to kill his brother.

Tien
Tien is one of the weaker fighters of DBZ like Yamcha. Tien is serious, determined to do stuff and tough. He never turned SSJ because, he has a bald head.

Trunks
There is two parts for Trunks, normal Trunks and future Trunks. Future Trunks is in the future fighting the androids while in the past Goku is fighting King Cold and Frieza. Future Trunks came and defeated Frieza and Cold, then he disappeared back to the future. Later on, Vegeta and Bulma had a baby which is Trunks.

Vegeta
Vegeta was first a bad guy and then turned into a good guy. After beating the Ginyu Force, Vegeta decided he cant beat Frieza alone so he joined the Z gang. He then be came a part of the group. At the Buu saga, Vegeta became a traitor because he decided to join Dabura to bacome Majin Vegeta.
Vegetto
Vegetto is Goku and Vegeta combined. This is the first time they combined. Vegetto had the highest power level in Dragonballz, a good level of 50,000,000!!! Vegetto damaged Buu so bad enough to weaken him a lot.

Yajirobe
(suggested by Brian Spears, bspears@shore.intercom.net)
This f at, funny, weird guy is also one of the characters that saved the day once. He had cut off Vegeta's tail and had cut Vegeta's armor. Yajirobe likes to eat eat eat. When he was at Kami's look-out, he just kept eating. Yajirobe is a desert Bandit.

Yamcha
Yamcha, like Tien, is weak fighter. He trained under Kami and has the same kind of uniform as Goku and Krillin.
